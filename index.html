About Me:

Liam English-Birge

For photography I focus on capturing the small details in the larger picture, trying to photograph things that one can easily miss. I enjoy photographing nature, animals, and people from unusual angles and distances, focusing on tiny details.
When I draw, I like to let my pen lead the way, letting shapes occur naturally and ideas flow freely. I enjoy drawing characters, etherial scenes, and things with tiny details.

The world has so much more to it than a passing glance can tell
